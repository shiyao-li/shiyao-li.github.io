About Me

I am currently a PhD student at IMAGINE (Institut Polytechnique de Paris) and Willow (Inria), supervised by Vincent Lepetit and Shizhe Chen, funded by the ERC Advanced Grant explorer.

My research interests lie primarily in 3D computer vision and embodied AI. Before my PhD, I graduated with highest honors (mention très bien) from Mathematics, Vision, and Learning (MVA) master program at ENS Paris Saclay.

Feel free to drop me an email if you'd like to discuss a potential collaboration.
